How to Make: Steak Quesadillas

Both a quick, easy meal and a perfect snack for you and a friend, steak quesadillas are the perfect pairing of melty cheese and savory beef. With a short prep time and amazing flavor, there's no reason you shouldn't have this recipe in your pocket!

Ingredients:

  • Skirt or flank steak
  • Mexican cheese blend
  • Flour tortillas
  • Oil
  • Seasoning: salt, pepper, garlic power, onion powder, cumin

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Prepare the steak by thoroughly drying off with paper towels. Season generously and let dry brine for at least 30 minutes and up to 24 hours. Once marinated, cook steak to preferred doneness and set aside to rest. After at least 5 minutes, slice steak into bite sized pieces. Be sure to cut with the grain to preserve tenderness.
  2. Step 2: In a separate pan over medium heat, add a little oil, the bottom tortilla, and the mexican blend cheese. Once the cheese begins to melt in the tortilla you may add the diced up steak before assembling with the top tortilla.
  3. Step 3: Flip the tortilla every 60-90 seconds until both sides of the quesadilla reach the preferred crispyness. Remove from heat and let rest.
  4. Step 4: Serve alongside some fresh guac or salsa and enjoy!
